Why Korean Dogs Need Your Help

In Korea, thousands of stray dogs struggle to survive on the streets. Due to cultural attitudes and limited resources, most will never find homes locally. International adoption is their only hope for a loving family.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to adopt? +

Adoption fees typically range from $500-$800, which covers health checks, vaccinations, spay/neuter surgery, microchipping, and flight costs from Korea to the US.

How long does the process take? +

The entire process usually takes 2-3 months from application to arrival. This includes application review, matching, health preparations, and flight arrangements.

Are the dogs healthy? +

Yes! All dogs receive comprehensive health checks, vaccinations, deworming, and are spayed/neutered before travel. They also undergo behavioral assessments to ensure they're ready for adoption.

Do you provide support after adoption? +

Absolutely! We provide lifetime support for all our adoptions. This includes training advice, behavioral support, and connections to local resources.
